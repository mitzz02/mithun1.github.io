QUEEN OF HILLS

Ooty, short for Ootacamund and officially known as Udhagamandalam, is a popular hill station located in the Nilgiri Hills of Tamil Nadu, India. It is known for its pleasant climate, tea gardens, lush greenery, and scenic landscapes. Ooty is a favorite destination for tourists seeking relaxation, nature walks, and adventure activities such as trekking and boating on Ooty Lake.

WildLife In Niligirs

"Explore the untamed wonders of the Nilgiris: where wilderness thrives and biodiversity flourishes."

The Nilgiri Biosphere Reserve, nestled in the Western Ghats of southern India, hosts diverse wildlife including the endangered Nilgiri Tahr, Indian Elephant, Gaur, Nilgiri Langur, Malabar Giant Squirrel, and Indian Leopard. This UNESCO World Network of Biosphere Reserves area is vital for conservation efforts and supports a unique ecosystem rich in biodiversity.
